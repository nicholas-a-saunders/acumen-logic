Refund Policy

Last updated: 3 February 2026

This policy explains the Acumen Logic 30-day Score Improvement Guarantee for the paid training programme.

30-Day Score Improvement Guarantee

The guarantee applies to score improvement in Acumen Logic assessments only. Your baseline is your free diagnostic score. Your comparison score is your paid-course mock assessment score.

What counts as score improvement

Score improvement means your overall mock score is higher than your baseline diagnostic score, measured by overall percentage score or total points.

This guarantee does not apply to job offers, hiring outcomes, or performance in third-party employer assessments.

Eligibility

To qualify for a refund under this guarantee, all of the following must apply:

  • You complete both the free diagnostic and the paid-course mock assessment within 30 days of purchase
  • Both attempts are valid completed attempts (not blank submissions or abandoned attempts)
  • Your mock score does not improve versus your diagnostic baseline
  • You submit your claim within 30 days of purchase

How to claim

Email enquiries@acumenlogic.co.uk with:

  • Your full name and purchase email
  • Your order number or transaction ID
  • Your diagnostic score and mock score

Remedy and processing

If your claim meets the eligibility criteria, we will issue a full refund to your original payment method. We aim to review claims within 3 business days and process approved refunds within 5-10 business days.

Important: Once a refund is processed, access to the paid course is removed.

Consumer Rights

This refund policy does not affect your statutory rights as a consumer under UK law, including the Consumer Rights Act 2015 and the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will not affect purchases made before the policy update.
