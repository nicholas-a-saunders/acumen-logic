Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 February 2026

Acumen Logic ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK data protection law.

Data controller: Acumen Logic. Contact: enquiries@acumenlogic.co.uk.

What we collect

  • Contact details: such as name and email address when you submit forms, use the diagnostic, or contact us.
  • Diagnostic data: your diagnostic responses and performance data, including overall score and category breakdown.
  • Purchase data: purchase details and transaction references provided to us through checkout partners.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, approximate location, browser/device information, and referring source via analytics tools.
  • Marketing preferences: whether you have opted in to receive marketing emails.

We do not provide website account creation and we do not collect or store passwords.

Checkout and payments

Checkout is provided by ThriveCart. Payments are processed by Stripe via ThriveCart.

Your full card details are entered directly into ThriveCart/Stripe environments and are not stored by our website. We receive only the information needed to fulfil your purchase, such as your name, email address, product purchased, and transaction reference details.

How we use your information

Purpose Legal basis (UK GDPR)
Providing diagnostic results and category feedback Performance of a contract / steps at your request
Processing purchases and sending service communications Performance of a contract
Analysing performance data to improve training content and delivery Legitimate interests
Sending marketing emails Consent (where you opt in)
Meeting legal and accounting obligations Legal obligation

Third parties and processors

  • ThriveCart: hosted checkout and order management.
  • Stripe (via ThriveCart): payment processing.
  • Google Analytics: website usage analytics.
  • Email/automation tools: may be used when enabled (for example, Kit/ConvertKit) for operational emails and consented marketing.
  • Hosting and technical providers: to run and secure the website.

We do not sell your personal data.

Cookies and analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies, including Google Analytics, to understand website usage and improve performance. For full cookie information, please see our Cookies Policy.

Where required, we rely on cookie consent controls for non-essential analytics cookies.

International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the UK. Where this happens, we use appropriate safeguards under UK data protection law (for example, approved contractual safeguards).

Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above. In practice, this usually means:

  • Purchase and transaction records retained for legal/accounting requirements (typically up to 6 years).
  • Diagnostic and related performance data retained while needed to provide results and improve training, then deleted or anonymised where appropriate.
  • Marketing data retained until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent.
  • Analytics data retained according to Google Analytics settings.

Your rights

You have rights under UK GDPR, including access, correction, erasure, restriction, objection, data portability, and withdrawal of consent (where processing is based on consent).

To exercise your rights, email enquiries@acumenlogic.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at enquiries@acumenlogic.co.uk.
