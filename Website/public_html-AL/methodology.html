The Challenge

Why most candidates fail finance assessments

Big 4 and investment banks don't test what you learned at university. They test how you perform under pressure with complex, unfamiliar data.

Data Overload

Multiple tables, conflicting metrics, and irrelevant data designed to overwhelm working memory. Most candidates attempt to hold everything in their head—and fail.

Context Switching

Rapid alternation between calculation types destroys rhythm and invites error. The test forces you to jump from percentages to ratios to logic without warning.

Precision Traps

Small misreads—percentage vs. percentage points, thousands vs. millions—result in confident wrong answers. The test rewards precision, not just speed.
