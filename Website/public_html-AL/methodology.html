Why Most Candidates Fail the Assessment Stage

Big 4 and investment banking assessments are engineered to trigger specific cognitive failures. They test your ability to integrate data across multiple tables while under severe time pressure—not just raw mathematical ability.

Data Overload

Multiple tables, conflicting metrics, and irrelevant data designed to overwhelm working memory.

Context Switching

Rapid alternation between calculation types that destroys rhythm and invites error.

Ambiguous Wording

Precise interpretation required—small misreads result in confident wrong answers.
