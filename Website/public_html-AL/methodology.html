How We Teach

Our Methodology

Assessment-aligned training built on structured decision-making

Assessment-Aligned Training

Every question, technique, and drill in the Acumen Logic programme mirrors the real assessments used by leading UK employers. We don’t teach generic maths—we train you on the exact question formats, time pressures, and cognitive demands of SHL, Aon, Korn Ferry, and Cappfinity aptitude tests.

Our methodology is built on three core principles that separate structured preparation from random practice.

01

Assessment Alignment

Every question type maps directly to SHL, Aon, Korn Ferry, and Cappfinity formats. You practise what you’ll face—not abstract puzzles or irrelevant exercises. The wording, data formats, and answer structures match real assessment conditions.

02

Structured Decision Trees

For every question type, we provide a repeatable decision framework. Instead of relying on intuition, you follow a systematic process: identify the question type, apply the correct method, verify, and move on. This eliminates guesswork under pressure.

03

Progressive Difficulty

Training begins with foundational skills and advances through increasingly complex, multi-step problems. Each module builds on the last, ensuring you develop both speed and accuracy before facing assessment-level complexity.

Category-Specific Methodology

Each aptitude category demands a distinct approach. Here’s how we train each one.

NR

Numerical Reasoning

Numerical reasoning questions test your ability to work with quantitative data quickly and accurately. Our methodology focuses on building speed arithmetic, percentage and ratio fluency, and structured approaches to multi-step calculations.

Percentages Ratios Speed Arithmetic Profit & Loss Currency Conversion
DI

Data Interpretation

Data interpretation questions present complex tables, charts, and multi-source datasets. We train systematic table reading, chart analysis, and multi-source data integration—teaching you to extract the right data before calculating.

Table Reading Chart Analysis Multi-Source Integration Data Extraction Trend Analysis
LR

Logical Reasoning

Logical reasoning questions assess your ability to identify patterns, apply rules, and draw valid conclusions. Our approach covers deductive reasoning, pattern recognition, and systematic rule application under timed conditions.

Deductive Reasoning Pattern Recognition Rule Application Syllogisms Sequencing

The 6-Module Structure

A progressive curriculum that builds from foundations to full assessment readiness

1

Foundations

Core arithmetic, mental maths shortcuts, and the fundamental techniques that underpin every question type. Builds the speed base for everything that follows.

2

Numerical Reasoning

Percentages, ratios, rates, profit & loss, and multi-step calculations. Structured decision trees for every NR question format used in real assessments.

3

Data Interpretation

Table reading, chart analysis, and multi-source data integration. Learn to extract the right data first, then calculate—eliminating the most common DI errors.

4

Logical Reasoning

Deductive reasoning, pattern recognition, rule application, and sequencing. Systematic frameworks for each LR question type that remove ambiguity.

5

Test Strategy

Time management, question triage, educated guessing, and error-checking techniques. The meta-skills that turn accuracy into a passing score under real conditions.

6

Pre-Assessment Review

Full-length mock assessments under timed conditions. Interleaved NR/DI/LR format mirrors the real test. Identifies remaining weak spots before your actual assessment.
