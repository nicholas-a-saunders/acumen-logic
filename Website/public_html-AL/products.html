Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products

What’s the difference between the training course and the benchmark platform?

The training course is a structured 6-module programme that teaches you the methodology behind every question type—numerical reasoning, data interpretation, logical reasoning, SJT, and mock assessments. The benchmark platform is a live testing environment where you take timed assessments and receive a percentile ranking against other finance candidates. They complement each other: the course teaches the skills, the platform measures your progress.

Can I use the benchmark platform without buying the training course?

Absolutely. The Free tier gives you 3 benchmarks per month with 19 questions each and a percentile ranking. You can upgrade to Pro or Elite at any time. The Elite plan includes full training course access bundled in.

Is the £199 training course price permanent?

The £199 price is our founding member rate. This is a one-time payment for lifetime access. The price will increase as we add more content and features, but founding members keep their rate locked in permanently.

Which employers and test providers does this cover?

Our content is aligned to the exact question types used by SHL, Aon (Cut-e), Korn Ferry, and Cappfinity—the test providers used by KPMG, Deloitte, PwC, EY, Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, Barclays, HSBC, Citi, Accenture, and more.
