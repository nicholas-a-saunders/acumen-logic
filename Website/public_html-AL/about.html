About Acumen Logic

Precision preparation for UK finance assessments

Who We Are

Acumen Logic was founded to solve a specific problem: candidates targeting Big 4, investment banking, and consulting roles were failing aptitude assessments not because they lacked ability, but because they lacked structured preparation.

We built a platform that combines assessment-aligned training with live percentile benchmarking. Our 6-module course covers every skill tested in SHL, Aon, Korn Ferry, and Cappfinity assessments, while our benchmark platform lets you see exactly where you rank against other candidates.

Every question template mirrors real assessment formats. Every percentile is calculated from real candidate data. Every module is structured around the decision trees and methodologies that produce results.

What We Cover

Three Core Assessment Categories

Every major UK finance employer tests these three areas. We train all of them.

Numerical Reasoning

Percentages, ratios, compound growth, and speed arithmetic. The most-tested category across SHL, Aon, and Korn Ferry assessments, mastered through structured decision trees and timed practice.

Data Interpretation

Tables, charts, multi-source integration, and mixed-unit calculations. The critical skill for Big 4 assessments where candidates must extract and synthesise data under severe time pressure.

Logical Reasoning

Deductive reasoning, syllogisms, pattern recognition, and rule application. Covers SHL Deductive and Korn Ferry Logical tests with systematic frameworks for consistent accuracy.
