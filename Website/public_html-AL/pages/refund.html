Refund Policy

Last updated: 3 February 2026

At Acumen Logic, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be requested for our digital products and services.

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on our online courses. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase date.

Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • Your refund request must be submitted within 14 days of the original purchase date
  • You must provide a valid reason for your refund request
  • You must not have completed more than 30% of the course content

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at hello@acumenlogic.co.uk with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Email address used for the purchase
  • Date of purchase
  • Order number or transaction ID
  • Reason for requesting a refund

Refund Processing

Once we receive your refund request, we will:

  • Review your request within 3 business days
  • Notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund
  • If approved, process your refund within 5-10 business days

Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Please note: Due to the digital nature of our products, once a refund is processed, your access to the course materials will be revoked immediately.

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Purchases made more than 14 days ago
  • Courses where more than 30% of the content has been accessed or completed
  • Products purchased during special promotional periods where "no refund" was clearly stated
  • Any bonus materials or add-ons provided free of charge

Subscription Services

If you have purchased a subscription-based service:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings
  • Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period
  • No partial refunds are provided for unused portions of a billing period
  • The 14-day money-back guarantee applies only to the first billing period of new subscriptions

Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or credit card company before contacting us to resolve the issue, we reserve the right to contest the chargeback and may take appropriate action including suspending your account access.

We encourage you to contact us first at hello@acumenlogic.co.uk to resolve any issues before initiating a chargeback.

Exceptions

We understand that exceptional circumstances may occur. If you believe you have a valid reason for a refund that falls outside of this policy, please contact us and we will review your case individually.

Consumer Rights

This refund policy does not affect your statutory rights as a consumer under UK law, including the Consumer Rights Act 2015 and the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will not affect purchases made before the policy update.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at:

Email: hello@acumenlogic.co.uk
