Index to Real Value
Hartwell's share price was £7.52 at the end of 2023. Using the index, what was the share price at the end of 2025?
Table C — Hartwell Share Price Index (Base Year 2022 = 100)
Year20212022202320242025
Share Price Index8810094108126
A
£9.48
B
£9.93
C
£10.08
D
£5.61

The index ratio from 2023 to 2025 is 126 ÷ 94. Share price: £7.52 × (126 ÷ 94) = £7.52 × 1.3404 = £10.08. Option A (£9.48) divides by the base year (100) instead of 2023's index: £7.52 × (126 ÷ 100) = £9.48 — the base year is irrelevant when you have a known price at a specific year. Option B (£9.93) treats the 32 index-point difference as 32%: £7.52 × 1.32 = £9.93 — but 32 index points from a base of 94 is 34.0%, not 32%. Option D (£5.61) inverts the ratio: £7.52 × (94 ÷ 126) — this gives the 2023 price if £7.52 were the 2025 price. This is the hardest index question type: converting a real value between two non-base years. Revisit: Lesson 3.6
